Booking Tips

Make your train booking experience better

Book Early: Tickets are available 120 days in advance. Book early for best availability.
Check Tatkal: For last-minute bookings, use Tatkal quota which opens one day before journey.
Verify Details: Always double-check passenger details before confirming booking.
Download E-Ticket: Save your e-ticket and carry valid ID proof during journey.
